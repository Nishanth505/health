Health Tips
Nutrition Tips
Eat the rainbow
— colorful fruits and veggies provide
a variety of nutrients.
Stay hydrated! Aim for 6–8 glasses of water daily.
Fitness Tips
Move your body every day
— even a 10-minute walk helps!
Stretch before bed to ease tension
and improve flexibility.
Sleep & Recovery Tips
Stick to a consistent sleep schedule,
even on weekends.
Avoid screens 30 minutes before bedtime.
